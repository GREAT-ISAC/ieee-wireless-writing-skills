# Venue: IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications

JSAC-style work should align with a special issue theme and show field-level significance within a precise technical scope.

Emphasize:

- Why the problem is central to the special issue topic.
- A stronger architecture or system-level perspective than a narrow module paper.
- Comparisons that include current high-end competitors.
- Discussion of deployment, scalability, or standardization relevance when the topic supports it.

Avoid broad visionary prose without technical evidence.
