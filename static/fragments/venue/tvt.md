# Venue: IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology

TVT-style drafts should make mobility, vehicular/channel dynamics, and deployment constraints visible.

Emphasize:

- Mobility model, Doppler, handover, V2X, UAV, railway, or vehicular channel relevance when applicable.
- Latency, reliability, tracking, and robustness under movement.
- Practical constraints such as feedback delay, localization error, sensing uncertainty, or traffic dynamics.

Avoid a generic wireless paper with "vehicular" added only in motivation.
