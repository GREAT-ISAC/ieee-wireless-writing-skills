# Venue: IEEE Wireless Communications

Treat this as a magazine-style venue, not a standard Transactions article.

Emphasize:

- Tutorial-style explanation for communications and networking readers.
- System insight, architecture, trends, open challenges, and deployment implications.
- Clear diagrams, taxonomy tables, design guidelines, and lessons learned.
- Policy, standardization, spectrum, ecosystem, protocol-stack, and service implications when relevant.
- Accessible technical depth: enough rigor to be credible, but avoid derivation-heavy presentation unless it directly supports insight.

Prefer:

- Survey/taxonomy framing with a clear takeaway.
- Problem-solution-roadmap structure.
- Cross-layer and system-level implications.
- Use-case driven examples and practical constraints.

Avoid:

- A narrow algorithm paper rewritten as a magazine article without broad insight.
- Dense theorem/proof or hyperparameter detail.
- Claiming maturity of AI/foundation-model systems without deployment, benchmark, or dataset evidence.
