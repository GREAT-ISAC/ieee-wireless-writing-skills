# Section: Experiments

Results prose should answer reviewer questions, not narrate plots chronologically.

Audit for this minimum content:

- Setup: channel/data, SNR, antenna count, bandwidth, mobility, training split, and implementation details.
- Baselines and fairness.
- Metrics.
- Main comparison.
- Ablation.
- Sensitivity or robustness.
- Complexity/latency if claimed.

State exact conditions for each gain. Do not generalize beyond the tested range. When evidence is missing, identify the missing experiment or detail and the claim that must be weakened until it exists; do not design a full protocol unless explicitly requested.
