# Area: RIS

RIS papers must state:

- RIS model: passive/active, continuous/quantized phase, amplitude coupling, hardware impairments, near-field/far-field.
- CSI acquisition path and cascaded-channel assumption.
- Optimization variables: BS beamforming, RIS phases, user scheduling, power, sensing waveform, or placement.
- Practical overhead: training, feedback, control signaling, and update rate.

Claims of practical RIS deployment need evidence beyond ideal continuous phase and perfect CSI.
