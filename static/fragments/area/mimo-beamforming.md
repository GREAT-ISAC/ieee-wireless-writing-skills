# Area: MIMO and Beamforming

MIMO and beamforming work must define:

- Antenna architecture: fully digital, hybrid, analog, massive MIMO, cell-free, distributed, or near-field.
- CSI assumption at transmitter and receiver.
- Objective: rate, SINR, energy efficiency, localization, secrecy, fairness, or sensing-communication tradeoff.
- Constraints: power, constant modulus, quantized phase, RF chains, fronthaul, feedback, latency.

Compare against optimization and communication baselines, not only neural baselines.
