# Area: ISAC

ISAC papers must keep communication and sensing objectives separate before discussing tradeoffs.

Specify:

- Communication metric: rate, SINR, outage, BER/BLER, latency, fairness.
- Sensing metric: CRB, detection probability, false alarm, range/velocity/angle error, tracking error.
- Waveform, beamforming, resource allocation, or receiver architecture.
- Tradeoff curve and operating points.

Do not claim "integrated" value unless the experiment shows the joint design beats separated or communication-only/sensing-only baselines under a fair resource budget.
