# Area: Wireless Datasets and Benchmarks

Dataset papers must make the data artifact auditable.

Specify:

- Data type: CSI, IQ, channel impulse response, channel matrix, spectrum sensing, localization, mobility trace, traffic log, packet trace, beam pair, sensing label, or network telemetry.
- Collection setup: hardware, antennas, bandwidth, carrier, sampling rate, environment, mobility, synchronization, calibration, and labeling process.
- Splits: cross-device, cross-location, cross-user, cross-day, cross-SNR, cross-cell, or cross-scenario as required by the claim.
- Leakage controls: session, location, user, environment, time, and preprocessing leakage.
- Metadata: license, consent/privacy, anonymization, data card, benchmark tasks, baseline scripts, and versioning.
- Baselines: strong classical and learning baselines with reproducible settings.

Do not claim generalization or foundation-model readiness unless the dataset scale, diversity, metadata, splits, and benchmark design support it.
