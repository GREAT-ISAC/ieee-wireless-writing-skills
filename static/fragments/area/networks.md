# Area: Wireless Networks

Wireless networking papers must specify:

- Network topology, traffic model, scheduling/resource allocation problem, interference model, and time scale.
- Objective: throughput, delay, reliability, fairness, energy, age of information, or utility.
- Baselines: greedy, convex/relaxed optimization, heuristic, queue-aware, RL, or protocol baselines as relevant.
- Evaluation: load, density, mobility, channel variation, and scalability.

Do not claim scalability without runtime, convergence, or large-network evidence.
