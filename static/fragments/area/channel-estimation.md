# Area: Channel Estimation

Channel-estimation papers must make pilot, channel, and error assumptions explicit.

Check:

- Pilot length and overhead.
- Channel model: sparse, correlated, time-varying, near-field, RIS cascaded, doubly selective, or wideband.
- Metric: NMSE, BER/BLER after detection, spectral efficiency, or downstream task performance.
- Baselines: LS, LMMSE/MMSE, OMP/CS, model-driven deep unfolding, or current neural estimators as appropriate.

When reporting NMSE, specify SNR, channel distribution, and whether the estimator uses perfect side information.
