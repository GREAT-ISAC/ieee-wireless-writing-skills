# Area: CSI

CSI work must specify:

- CSI type: raw complex CSI, amplitude/phase, delay-Doppler, angular-domain, subcarrier features, or estimated channel matrix.
- Acquisition: pilots, feedback, compression, calibration, synchronization, or hardware source.
- Preprocessing: phase sanitization, denoising, interpolation, normalization, truncation, or augmentation.
- Scope: environment, device, bandwidth, antenna count, subcarriers, mobility, and train/test split.

Do not claim robustness across domains unless cross-device, cross-location, cross-user, or cross-time evidence exists.
