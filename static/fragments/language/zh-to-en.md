# Language: Chinese to English

Translate argument, not clause order.

Common repairs:

- Replace "with the development of" openings with the exact wireless pressure or bottleneck.
- Convert "has important significance" into a specific technical reason.
- Move limitations before proposed method when introducing a gap.
- Turn long Chinese sentences into two or three IEEE-style sentences.
- Replace vague "better performance" with metric-specific evidence.
- Preserve domain terms: CSI, OFDM, MIMO, RIS, ISAC, SNR, SINR, NMSE, BER, BLER, spectral efficiency.

When the Chinese draft hides missing evidence, do not smooth it over. Mark the missing figure, citation, or experiment.
